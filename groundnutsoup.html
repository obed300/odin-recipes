GROUNDNUT SOUP

Description of groundnut soup.

Groundnut soup is also known as peanut soup but in Ghana it is mostly called groundnut soup. It is one of the popular soups in Ghana because it can be used with several foods such as rice, fufu, gari, konkonte, etc. Another factor that makes it popular among Ghanaians is it afordability. With as low as 10 Ghana cedis it can be used to prepare this type of soup which make most people like it in Ghana.

Ingredients used in preparing groundnut soup

  • Peanut butter paste
  • Pounds of Chicken
  • Tomatoes and tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps in preparing Groundnut Soup.

  1. Wash the chicken and season it with an onion, Garlic, ginger, salt and/or any chicken seasoning spice that you prefer to use.
  2. Blend tomatoes, another onion, and pepper
  3. Include the blended vegetables to the pot of chicken and let it boil for some minutes. Allow it to cook until the meat is tender.
  4. You can add more water to it if you think its too tick.
  5. Allow the soup to boil gently for about 20min.
  6. Add sault to it and then taste it if its fine for you.
  7. For you to know if the soup is ready, you'll spot some oil on the surface.
