Jollof Rice

Description of Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a delicacy enjoyed by many Africans and non-Africans alike. Ghanaians are arguably one the best lovers of this meal. This is a delicious jollof rice recipe which is a combination of unique Ghanaian flavors. This recipe is made with chicken on the side. It can be eaten with a salad or spaghetti on the side as well.

Steps to Prepare Jollof Rice.

  1. Wash the chicken
  2. Season with salt, garlic and ginger
  3. Steam the chicken for about 10 minutes/ till it is done
  4. Allow the chicken to cool down for a few minutes
  5. Fry the chicken
  6. Once the chicken is done, remove it from the oil and strain
  7. Set aside
  8. Pour the oil (about 5 ounces) into a cooking dish
  9. Once the temperature of the oil increases, add sliced onions, diced garlic and sliced small green peppers to the oil
  10. Once the onion begins to brown a little, add the tomato puree and stir
  11. Add blended fresh tomato
  12. Allow to simmer until the water in the tomatoes begin to dry (it begins to form a stew)
  13. Add the spices
  14. When it forms a stew, break the smoked washed mackerel and add it to the stew
  15. After about thirty seconds, add the chicken stock; add a little more water if the chicken stock is not enough to cook the rice.
  16. Taste for salt and adjust as required
  17. Add the rice to the jollof stock you have prepared and reduce the heat.
  18. Allow it to simmer on low heat
  19. After a few minutes, add the veggies to the rice and cover with a foil or a clean napkin before covering with the lid
  20. Allow to simmer until the rice is cooked.
  21. One the rice is cooked, your Jollof is ready.
  22. Serve with chicken on the side
